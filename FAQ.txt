Joins+ Bot not responding?

If the Joins+ Bot is not responding it could have multiple reasons. 
**Here are the most common reasons:**
**1**. The bot doesn't have enough permissions to send messages and embeds.
To fix this issue, give the bot a role with administrator permissions. Or use the command `-invite` then kick the bot and add it with the permissions it needs to your guild.
**2**. The bot is offline. First, check if the bot is actually offline. You can check that by clicking on the profile picture of the Joins+ bot and checking if the dot bottom right is gray or green.
